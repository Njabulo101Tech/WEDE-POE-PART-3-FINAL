# WEDE-POE-PART-3-FINAL

1. Project Overview
MT Print.co is a modern printing company offering high-quality printing solutions for businesses and individuals, including business cards, flyers, banners, promotional materials, and custom printing services. To strengthen its online presence, reach more clients, and improve accessibility, MT Print.co requires a professional, responsive website.
Project Goal:
Design and develop a fully functional website for MT Print.co that showcases its services, provides an easy way for clients to request quotes, and integrates contact and location information.
________________________________________
2. Objectives
1.	Develop a visually appealing, modern website reflecting MT Print.co’s brand.
2.	Ensure easy navigation for users to explore services.
3.	Provide a platform for clients to request quotes and contact the company.
4.	Showcase past work through a gallery and testimonials.
5.	Optimize the website for mobile devices and search engines (SEO).
________________________________________
3. Scope of Work
Website Features:
•	Home Page: Company overview, services summary, featured promotions.
•	About Us: Company history, mission, vision, and goals.
•	Services: Detailed descriptions of all printing services.
•	Portfolio/Gallery: Showcase of completed projects.
•	Contact Us: Contact form, Google Maps integration, phone, email, and office address.
•	Quote Request Form: Clients submit project details to receive cost estimates.
•	Blog (Optional): Articles about printing tips, company updates, and promotions.
•	SEO Optimization: On-page SEO to improve search visibility.
•	Responsive Design: Optimized for desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
Technology Stack:
•	Front-end: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap/Tailwind
•	Back-end: PHP or Node.js (for forms and dynamic content)
•	Database: MySQL (to store quote requests)
•	Other Tools: Google Maps API, SEO tools, contact form plugins
